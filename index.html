About Kayla's Customs

Founded by Michaela Howe, Kayla's Customs is a dedicated home-based tyre service workshop located at 27 Beihlers Road, Weymouth, Auckland. We pride ourselves on delivering high-quality, reliable, and efficient tyre services to our local community.

Our commitment extends beyond just excellent service; we are deeply passionate about maintaining a safe environment for everyone on our premises and ensuring responsible disposal of used tyres. Our workshop is equipped to handle a range of tyre-related needs, basic vehicle servicing, and advanced diagnostics.

At Kayla's Customs, you're not just a customer; you're part of our commitment to safety and quality.
